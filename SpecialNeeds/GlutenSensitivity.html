Gluten Sensitivity / Celiac Disease
Gluten is a substance found in wheat, barley and some other grains. It is the substance that makes dough springy and stretchy. Without it, it is difficult to make palatable breads, etc.
Sometimes, getting around this can be as easy as using a gluten-free flour… but usually not. Usually it involves using a different mix of ingredients and different mixing and/or baking instructions.
