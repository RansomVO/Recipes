Lactose Intolerance

Lactose is a type of sugar found in Dairy items such as Milk, Cream, Butter, etc.
Unfortunately, Dairy items are common ingredients in many recipes.

Common Substitutions

For many of our recipes, we can just do a basic substitution. This will give you a starting spot. If it doesn't work, make a note of it so you don't make the same mistake twice.

Ingredient Substitution Notes
Milk Almond Milk Use about 15%-20% less than the recipe calls for.
Coconut Milk Use about 15%-20% less than the recipe calls for.
Rice Milk Use about 15%-20% less than the recipe calls for.
Cream Coconut Cream This is a non-sweetened, very thick substance.
Evaporated Milk
Butter Coconut Oil Pretty much works just like butter.
